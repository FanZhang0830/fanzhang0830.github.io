About

I specialize in geospatial analytics, remote sensing, hydrological modeling, river ice analysis, and environmental decision-support tools. My work focuses on transforming spatial and environmental datasets into actionable intelligence.

Featured Project: EcoHydroAI

EcoHydroAI is a WebGIS-based environmental intelligence prototype that integrates open remote sensing layers, watershed datasets, and AI-assisted spatial analytics to support water quality monitoring and watershed decision-making.

  • Interactive WebGIS interface for watershed-level exploration
  • Integration of open basemaps, satellite imagery layers, and environmental datasets
  • Roadmap for AI-assisted anomaly detection and water quality insights
